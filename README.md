# On My Way Home 🎃

It's Halloween. It's the last day of school. All you want to do is get home, 
grab your costume and go trick-or-treating with your little sister and your friends.

But your sister's bike got stolen — and it's probably those hoodlums from the Skull Gang again.

You're Chino, 12 years old, and you've got a whole town to explore before the night is over.
Helping you along the way: your little sister Lil Sis, your friend Toni, and your loyal dog 
Good Boi.

And if the rumors are true, the ghost of a young kid haunts the graveyard after dark ...

---

**On My Way Home** is a Halloween-themed JRPG set in the sleepy small town of Basswood. 
Inspired by the games I grew up loving — think Earthbound, think SNES — it's a solo 
learning project where I'm building everything from scratch: the code, the art, the design.

Forests, graveyards, aliens, witches, and one very annoying gang of kids in skull masks. 
Basswood has secrets. Go find them.
